What is the capacitive reactance?
The capacitive reactance is the measurement of the opposition to alternating current by the capacitor.
How to calculate capacitive reactance?
To calculate capacitive reactance you should use the following formula:
x = 1 / (2 * π * f * c)
or
x = 1 / (ω * c)
where:
x = capacitive reactance
c = capacitor
w = angular frequency = 2 * π * f
Why use capacitive reactance?
The capacitive reactance is used in order to reduece the effects of any unwanted ripple voltage.
